Frequently Asked Questions

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is technology that enables computers to perform tasks that usually require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

Can AI replace teachers?

No. AI supports teachers by reducing repetitive tasks and providing personalized learning experiences. Teachers remain essential for guidance, motivation, and emotional support.

How does AI help students?

AI provides instant feedback, adaptive learning paths, smart tutoring, language translation, and access to educational resources from anywhere.

What skills will future students need?

Critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, digital literacy, and the ability to work effectively with AI technologies.
